01. Raw Data in Google Sheets

Dump the chaos.

Just drop your raw exports from Meta, TikTok, and YouTube into Google Sheets. The system automatically detects platforms and unifies the schema without manual formatting.

02. Validation & KPI Engine

Clean Data. Clear Business Impact.

The built-in logic scans for taxonomy errors (wrong dates, invalid objectives) and auto-calculates business metrics like CPV and CPC. Red cells tell you what to fix; Green cells mean you're ready.

03. Auto-Generate to Google Slides

The Magic Transition.

With one click, perfectly clean data is injected into your Google Slides template. Campaigns are grouped by date and product, fully formatted and ready to present.
